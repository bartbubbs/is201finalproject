Today's Leading Korean Boy Band - An Introduction

Who are StrayKids?

Straykids are a 8-member male Kpop Group. Debuting in 2018 with their hit "Hellevator" they have now grown to be one of the biggest groups within the kpop community with over 125 songs, their most streamed song being "Gods Menu" relesed in June 2020.

Straykids are divided into 3 sub-units consisting of: "3Racha" the production team, "DanceRacha" the dance team, and "VocalRacha" the vocal team. The unit names were created by 3racha members Bangchan, Han, and Changbin, who chose the name 3racha as it closely resembles Sriracha sauce, symbolizing their spice and unique flavor within the Kpop Community.
